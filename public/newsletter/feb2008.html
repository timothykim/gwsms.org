    Group class this week is on Saturday, Feb. 16!

  2. 2.

    We hope you are all looking forward to the 2008 SAGWA String Festival! The String Festival will be held on April 12th and 13th. Our fantastic clinicians are violin: Terry Durbin (CA), Nancy Lokken (MN), Jim Van Reeth (OH); and cello: Amy Barston (NY). This event includes several group classes on Saturday morning and afternoon with a clinician and other local teachers, and then the concert at the prestigious Strathmore Music Center in North Bethesda, MD, from 2:00-4:00 pm.

    To learn more please visit the String Festival Homepage.
